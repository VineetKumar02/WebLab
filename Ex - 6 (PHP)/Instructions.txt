1) Place the Folder "Database_connection" in :-
C:\xampp\htdocs

2) Start XAMPP and start the Apache & MySQL.

3) Open Apache Admin to see list of folders in htdocs. Open "Database_connection".
					(or)
   Navigate to your browser and type in “localhost/Database_connection/” in the address bar to view the output.

4) Open MySQL admin to look at database.
